FAQ

Do I need a Chromebook to use Chrome Canvas?

No. The website can be accessed across multiple devices such as a laptop, tablet and even your phone (if it's a smart phone).

Can I use Chrome Canvas on my phone?

Yes. Though all my tutorials are done on a computer, the same instructions should work on your smart phone.

Is there an official help site for Chrome Canvas?

Yes. Clickhere to visit the site.

Do I have to pay for Chrome Canvas?

No. It is completely, totally, 100% FREE.

Can I only use Chrome Canvas in Google Chrome?

No. Its cross-browser compatible. Meaning, it works in any web browser such as Edge, Opera, Firefox, etc.

What are other uses for Chrome Canvas besides drawing?

It works great as a whitetboard for online teachers and as a note pad if you need to quickly draw or jot something down.

What is an active stylus/pen?

It's a pen that has electronic components which allows users to write directly onto the display of a touchscreen device.
